About Our Club

Fostering comprehensive development through life skills education

Our Vision

To create a generation of confident, skilled, and socially responsible students who can navigate life's challenges with resilience and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

The Jeevan Kaushal Club at KITS Guntur is dedicated to empowering students with essential life skills that complement their technical education. We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who excel not only in academics but also in personal and professional spheres.

Club Inauguration

Established on December 23, 2021, our club has been actively working towards holistic student development through various programs, workshops, and activities designed to enhance life skills and personal growth.

Our Objectives

  • Develop communication and interpersonal skills
  • Foster leadership qualities and team building
  • Enhance emotional intelligence and self-awareness
  • Provide career guidance and professional development
  • Promote mental health and wellness
  • Create a supportive peer learning environment
