Building Inner Strength Through Yoga

A Wellness Initiative | B.Tech 2025 Induction Programme, KKR & KSR Institute of Technology & Sciences

Mr. V. Devi Srikanth

Software Engineer & Yoga Practitioner

As part of the B.Tech 2025 Induction Programme at KKR & KSR Institute of Technology & Sciences, we had the privilege of welcoming Mr. V. Devi Srikanth. His inspiring session introduced students to the art of yoga as a pathway to mental clarity, sharper focus, and inner resilience — essential strengths for thriving in today’s competitive world.

Core Tenets of the Session

Exploring the key principles discussed for holistic student development.

Mindful Foundations

The session began by introducing the core concepts of yoga as a tool for self-discovery. Students learned how consistent practice cultivates self-discipline, inner peace, and heightened self-awareness, creating a stable foundation for personal and academic growth.

Physical & Mental Resilience

Mr. Srikanth emphasized the importance of maintaining physical flexibility and balance as a gateway to mental resilience. Practical strategies were shared for improving concentration and managing stress, equipping students to navigate challenges with a calm and focused mind.

Academic & Personal Harmony

A key outcome of the event was motivating students to integrate yoga and mindfulness into their daily routines. The talk provided actionable tips on balancing demanding academic schedules with a regular yoga practice, promoting holistic development and sustained well-being.
