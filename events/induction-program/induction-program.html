Guest Lectures & Expert Talks

As part of the induction, we organize insightful guest lectures where experts from various fields share their knowledge and inspire our new students.

Mr. G. Lakshmaiah DSP, Guntur

An enlightening session on "Shaping the Students as Self-Disciplined Professionals and Responsible Citizens of India," emphasizing the importance of discipline and civic duty.

Dr. A.V. Raghava Business Entrepreneur & Social Activist

A motivational talk on "You are the Architect of Your Life," encouraging students to take ownership of their future and create their own success stories.

Dr. Kota Suresh Kumar Prof & HOD of Psychiatry, Katuri Medical College

A critical session addressing "Mobile and Drug Abuse Safeguard," raising awareness about the risks of digital addiction and substance abuse while promoting mental well-being.

Mr. Thota Ragava Rao Motor Vehicle Inspector

An essential lecture on "Road Safety," covering vital traffic rules and the importance of responsible behavior on the road to prevent accidents and save lives.

Dr. L. Mohan Baskhar

A powerful talk on "You Too Can Save Lives," focused on equipping students with knowledge of first aid, emergency response, and the life-saving impact of blood donation.

A Vibrant Club Culture

The induction program is the perfect platform for freshers to explore the diverse and dynamic club culture at KITS. It provides a unique opportunity for new students to discover a spectrum of interests, interact directly with senior members, and align with their personal and professional goals.

Jeevan Kaushal Club

"Nurturing Skills for Life."

Our club is dedicated to the holistic development of students by equipping them with essential life skills, fostering leadership, and promoting personal growth outside the academic curriculum.

  • Senior-Junior Interaction programs to guide and mentor new students.
  • Organizing insightful Guest Lectures from industry experts and academicians.
  • Workshops on essential life skills for navigating college and future careers.

Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell)

"Ideate. Innovate. Incubate."

The E-Cell fosters a startup culture and provides students with the resources to transform their innovative ideas into viable business ventures. We focus on creating a complete ecosystem, from initial idea validation to mentorship from successful founders.

  • Hosts idea pitching competitions and startup bootcamps.
  • Connects students with a network of mentors, experts, and investors.
  • Conducts workshops on business planning, finance, and marketing.

Innovation Club

"Where Curiosity Meets Creation."

The Innovation Club is where theoretical knowledge becomes tangible reality. We provide a platform for students to apply their engineering skills to real-world problems through hands-on, project-based learning in a collaborative environment.

  • Facilitates collaborative projects in fields like Robotics, AI, and IoT.

National Service Scheme (NSS)

"Not Me, But You."

The NSS unit connects our students with society, developing social consciousness and a spirit of selfless service. Our motto emphasizes that an individual's welfare is ultimately dependent on the welfare of the community as a whole.

  • Organizes community service events like blood donation camps.
  • Leads environmental initiatives such as tree plantation drives.
  • Conducts social awareness programs in local communities.

National Cadet Corps (NCC)

"Unity and Discipline."

The NCC is instrumental in shaping students into disciplined, patriotic, and responsible leaders. It aims to create a pool of organized, trained, and motivated youth to serve the nation in various capacities, both in military and civilian life.

  • Provides basic military training, including drill and map reading.
  • Instills discipline and patriotism through parades and national events.
  • Organizes adventure camps to build courage and leadership.
